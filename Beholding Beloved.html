Our Story

Our journey with the Devadasi women of Karnataka began in 2012, when our church sent a six-person team on a short-term mission trip to a small rural region in northern Karnataka. What we witnessed there — lives marked by hardship, yet full of resilience — left a permanent imprint on our hearts. In the face of deep injustice and cultural marginalization, we saw women with immeasurable worth in God’s eyes. We knew then that this was not a one-time trip, but the beginning of a long-term commitment.

In 2014, what was once called the “Devadasi Conference” was renamed the Beloved Conference, a name that reflects our core belief: these women are not defined by their past or social labels — they are God’s beloved daughters. Since then, the Beloved Conference has become a sacred space for healing, worship, teaching, and celebration. Over the past ten years, more than 1,000 women have attended the conference, many returning year after year, finding not only support and encouragement, but also encountering the love of Jesus.

Today, we remain deeply committed to this calling. Through ongoing ministry, partnership with local leaders, and the continued gathering of the Beloved Conference, we carry a bold vision: to reach all 80,000 Devadasi women in Karnataka with the hope and restoration found in Christ.

Each woman matters. Each story matters.

And our mission is to ensure that every single one hears: You are seen. You are loved. You are God’s Beloved.
